Tianqi Li (pronunciation: [tɪ-entʃi: ‘li:]) is a second-year Ph.D. student in [Department of Economics](https://econ.unc.edu/) in [University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill](https://www.unc.edu/) in the United States. His current research interests include Microeconometrics Theory and Application, Urban Economics, Nonparametric Estimation, Machine Learning.

In 2019, He achieved his bachelor’s degree in Mathematical Economics (Double major in Economics and Mathematics) in [School of Economics and Management](http://ems.whu.edu.cn/) in [Wuhan University](https://en.whu.edu.cn/index.htm) in China. His advisor for undergraduate dissertation (High-speed railway for Econ and LSTM network for Math) was Professor [Jingbo Cui](https://sites.google.com/site/jbcui2013).
